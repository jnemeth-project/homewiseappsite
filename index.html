The story

Why HomewiseApp?

Every household deserves a simple way to stay organised - without the chaos of group chats and scattered lists.

HomewiseApp was born from the everyday reality of shared living. Whether you're flatmates, a family, or partners under one roof, keeping track of who needs what, when things are happening, and what's for dinner shouldn't require a project manager. We're building the app we wished existed - thoughtfully designed, private by default, and genuinely useful from day one.
